FBLA National Competition

We are fundraising money to attend Nationals Competitions in Aneheim, CA from June 29 - July 2, 2017. We are the Future Business Leaders of America club at Palisade High School, Colorado with 20 members who have been preparing in business-related competitive events since September, including E-business, Social Media Campaign, Business Plan, Job Interview, and Digital Video Production. This money will be used to offset costs of members who qualify at both the District and State level in their events, to pay for the National Leadership Conference registration and hotel fees, and give them a chance to present their events at the National Level, a dream come true for many.

Member Deadline

The money will be split evenly between qualifying members and fundraising will end May 5, 2017, when registration fees will have to be paid for attending members. For many of our members, even if they qualify, will only be able to afford the conference through working jobs and fundraising. Many members have been fundraising on their own for two summers to attend the conference, help offset remaining costs for commited business kids and give them an opportunity of a lifetime!
