About Us Greetings! From Your 2016 FBLA Members

Throughout the year, our members engage in community service, battle it out at dodgeball during monthly lock-ins, and develop different skills relating to business and entrepreneurship! They are ready to stand out in our business competitive events, first competing at Districts, and then at State. This year, many of these members take on the challenge to try to get to Nationals, and they need YOUR help to make that happen, for a small donation, you are helping them achieve, compete and learn at an invaluable business experience.

Find out a little about who your donations are impacting!

Connect With Us

As an online yard-sale, we sell all our items "as is." We are not able to accept returns at this time, and once a purchase has been made, the sale is final. We welcome any questions regarding our policy through the Contact Us Form at the bottom of our page.
